Profile

Deep learning researcher specializing in robust and efficient AI for heterogeneous healthcare data.

Joy Dhar is a Ph.D. scholar in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Ropar. His work focuses on multimodal fusion, adversarial robustness, uncertainty-aware learning, and computationally efficient model design for medical AI systems that must remain useful under noise, shift, and infrastructure constraints.

Research Mission

Move beyond benchmark wins toward clinically dependable AI.

The emphasis is not only on accuracy, but also on model behavior under uncertainty, attack, distribution shift, missing modalities, and real deployment constraints. The target outcome is decision-support AI that remains useful in environments where data abundance and compute are not guaranteed.
