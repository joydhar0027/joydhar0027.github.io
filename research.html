Research

Designing AI systems that stay robust, efficient, and clinically relevant under multimodal complexity.

The core research agenda combines multimodal learning, robustness analysis, domain generalization, and efficient architecture design for healthcare applications.

Multimodal and Generative Models

Multimodal fusion, multi- and cross-modal attention, transformers, visual-language models, and generative modeling for medical data integration.

Robustness and Reliability Analysis

Adversarial attacks, empirical and certified defenses, uncertainty quantification, and curriculum-aware training strategies.

Domain Adaptation and Distillation

Knowledge distillation, modality-agnostic learning, and domain generalization for transfer across settings and sources.

Learning Foundations and Architectures

Supervised, self-supervised, unsupervised, continual, evidential, multitask, and physics-inspired learning approaches.

Biomedical AI Targets

Cost-effective medical image analysis, EHR and time-series modeling, and multi-disease classification and segmentation.

System Constraints

Model design grounded in practical compute limits, missing modalities, and the realities of healthcare environments with limited resources.

Does a Hybrid Space-Aware Weight Refiner Improve Multimodal Fusion Robustness?

Punjab, India | Feb. 2026

Robust-Zero: A Defense Framework for Zero-Shot Classification

Punjab, India | May 2025

Jointly uses random filtering, attention-noise injection, and disentangled feature representations to mitigate vulnerabilities in zero-shot settings.

Research Methods in Use

  • Attention-based multimodal fusion
  • Hybrid convolutional architectures
  • Uncertainty-aware optimization
  • Adversarial defense design
  • Knowledge distillation and transfer

Why This Agenda Matters

Healthcare AI often fails where data quality, modality coverage, and infrastructure support are weakest. This research direction treats robustness, efficiency, and transferability as first-order design goals rather than afterthoughts.
